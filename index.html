Portfolio

  • Japan Railways(Oyama - Okiwasure - Train Building Limit Checker)

    This is an IOS application used internally for Japan Railways Oyama. Its function is to check railway obstructions, given an image of the railway, and simulate the distances between possible obstructions and the train.

  • Japan Railways(Shinjuku - Equipment Tracking and Inventory)

    An application intended for tracking equipment used by JR's personnel and serves as inventory for the equipments, using an RFID tag on the equipments and is scanned using an RFID scanner. The scanner connects to the iOS device using BlueTooth Low Energy (BLE) .

  • Japan Railways(Sendai - TDView)

    Similar to JR Oyama, internally used in Sendai and also does railway simulation. This app includes plotting of different railway installations such as crossigns, LED posts, etc., aiding engineers in installation planning. It aims to aid engineers in visualising the placements of railway installations, and train drivers in visualising installation distances relative to their Point Of View.

  • ReviewBuzz

    Mobile app for ReviewBuzz. The app is a utility tool for the client's employees where they keep track of their performance, reviews and even redeem rewards based on the points they garner. Recently, it has added a new feature called BuzzPay, where in-field employees can receive payment directly from the customer through their cards using a card reader (using CardConnect's reader). The reader connects to the device using BLE. More information about the app here .
